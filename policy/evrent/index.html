Privacy Policy - Evrent

Last updated: 21 February 2026

1. Introduction

Evrent is a mobile application designed to help users manage events, rental inventory, and catering operations directly on their device.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Evrent handles data when you use the application.

2. Data Collection

Evrent does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data on external servers.

The application operates primarily offline and does not require user account creation.

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers

No data is transmitted to the developer or any third party.

3. Local Data Storage

All data entered into Evrent is stored locally on your device using a local database.

This may include:

  • Event information
  • Client details entered by the user
  • Rental inventory data
  • Catering menus
  • Financial or planning information

This data remains fully under your control and is accessible only on your device.

4. Backup and Google Drive Integration

Evrent may allow users to create backups stored in their personal Google Drive account.

Important notes:

  • Backups are initiated manually by the user.
  • Data is stored in the user's own Google Drive space.
  • The developer of Evrent does not access, view, or store these backups.
  • Google Drive usage is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Users are responsible for managing access permissions to their Google account.

5. Data Sharing

Evrent does not share user data with any third parties.

Since no external servers are used, your information is not transmitted outside your device except when you explicitly export or back up your data.

6. Data Security

Because data is stored locally, security depends primarily on your device's security measures, including:

  • Device lock or biometric protection
  • Operating system security
  • Google account protection (for backups)

Users are responsible for securing access to their own devices and accounts.

7. Data Deletion

Users can delete their data at any time by:

  • Deleting data inside the application, or
  • Uninstalling the application from their device.

Uninstalling Evrent removes locally stored data unless it has been backed up externally by the user.

8. Children's Privacy

Evrent is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. The application does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future if application features change.

Any updates will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.
